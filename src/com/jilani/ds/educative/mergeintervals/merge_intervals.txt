5. Minimum Meeting Rooms (hard) #
	Given a list of intervals representing the start and end time of ‘N’ meetings, find the minimum number of rooms required to hold all the meetings.
	
	a) Given a list of intervals, find the point where the maximum number of intervals overlap.
	b) Given a list of intervals representing the arrival and departure times of trains to a train station, 
		our goal is to find the minimum number of platforms required for the train station so that 
		no train has to wait.
